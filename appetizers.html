1. Crispy Spring Rolls

Ingredients:

  • 10 spring roll wrappers
  • 200g cabbage, shredded
  • 100g carrots, julienned
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon soy sauce
  • 1 teaspoon sesame oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Oil for frying

Steps:

  1. Heat a pan with oil and saut√© garlic until fragrant.
  2. Add cabbage and carrots, stir-fry for 3-4 minutes.
  3. Season with soy sauce, sesame oil, salt, and pepper. Let cool.
  4. Place filling on spring roll wrapper and roll tightly, sealing edges with water.
  5. Deep fry spring rolls until golden brown and crispy.
  6. Drain on paper towels and serve hot with sweet chili sauce.

2. Stuffed Mushrooms

Ingredients:

  • 12 large button mushrooms
  • 100g cream cheese
  • 50g breadcrumbs
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 50g parmesan cheese, grated
  • 2 tablespoons fresh parsley, chopped
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps:

  1. Preheat oven to 375¬∞F (190¬∞C).
  2. Remove mushroom stems and chop them finely.
  3. Mix chopped stems with cream cheese, breadcrumbs, garlic, parsley, and half the parmesan.
  4. Season the mixture with salt and pepper.
  5. Stuff each mushroom cap with the mixture.
  6. Place on a baking sheet, drizzle with olive oil, and sprinkle remaining parmesan.
  7. Bake for 20-25 minutes until golden and bubbling.
  8. Serve warm as a delicious appetizer.
