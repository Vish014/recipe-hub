1. Classic Chocolate Brownies

Ingredients:

  • 200g dark chocolate, chopped
  • 175g butter
  • 3 large eggs
  • 250g sugar
  • 75g all-purpose flour
  • 30g cocoa powder
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • Pinch of salt

Steps:

  1. Preheat oven to 350¬∞F (180¬∞C). Line a 9x9 inch baking pan with parchment paper.
  2. Melt chocolate and butter together in a double boiler, stirring until smooth.
  3. In a separate bowl, whisk eggs and sugar until light and fluffy.
  4. Add vanilla extract to the egg mixture.
  5. Fold in the melted chocolate mixture.
  6. Sift flour, cocoa powder, and salt together, then fold into the batter.
  7. Pour batter into prepared pan and bake for 25-30 minutes.
  8. Let cool completely before cutting into squares.

2. Vanilla Panna Cotta

Ingredients:

  • 500ml heavy cream
  • 100g sugar
  • 1 vanilla bean (or 2 teaspoons vanilla extract)
  • 2 teaspoons gelatin powder
  • 3 tablespoons cold water
  • Fresh berries for topping
  • Mint leaves for garnish

Steps:

  1. Sprinkle gelatin over cold water in a small bowl and let it bloom for 5 minutes.
  2. Split vanilla bean lengthwise and scrape out the seeds.
  3. In a saucepan, combine cream, sugar, and vanilla (both seeds and pod).
  4. Heat over medium heat, stirring until sugar dissolves. Do not boil.
  5. Remove from heat and discard vanilla pod.
  6. Add bloomed gelatin and stir until completely dissolved.
  7. Pour mixture into serving glasses or ramekins.
  8. Refrigerate for at least 4 hours or overnight until set.
  9. Top with fresh berries and mint leaves before serving.
